# Client Guide for Website Generator Application

## Introduction

Welcome to the Website Generator Application! This guide will help you create and manage your website using our easy-to-use platform. As a client, you can select themes and layouts, edit content, and manage your website without needing technical expertise.

## Getting Started

### Logging In

1. Navigate to the login page at `/auth/login`
2. Enter your email and password
3. You will be redirected to your client dashboard

### Client Dashboard Overview

Your dashboard provides access to all your website management tools:

- **My Websites**: View and manage your websites
- **Create New Website**: Start the process of creating a new website
- **Account Settings**: Manage your account information
- **Help & Support**: Access support resources

## Creating a Website

### Step 1: Select a Theme

1. From your dashboard, click "Create New Website"
2. Browse through the available themes
3. Click on a theme to see more details
4. Click "Select Theme" when you find one you like

### Step 2: Choose a Layout

1. After selecting a theme, you'll be shown compatible layouts
2. Browse through the available layouts
3. Click on a layout to see more details
4. Click "Select Layout" when you find one you like

### Step 3: Preview Your Website

1. After selecting a layout, you'll see a preview of your website
2. This preview shows how your website will look with the selected theme and layout
3. If you're not satisfied, you can go back and select a different theme or layout
4. If you're happy with the preview, click "Generate Website"

### Step 4: Basic Setup

1. Enter your website details:
   - Website name
   - Domain (if applicable)
   - Basic information
2. Click "Create Website" to finalize

## Editing Your Website

### Accessing the Content Manager

1. From your dashboard, select the website you want to edit
2. Click "Edit Content" to open the Content Manager

### Adding Components

1. In the Content Manager, click "Add Component"
2. Select the type of component you want to add:
   - Header
   - Paragraph
   - Image
   - Button
   - Hero Section
   - Image Gallery
   - Contact Form
3. Choose the position for your component
4. Click "Add Component" to add it to your page

### Editing Components

1. In the Content Manager, hover over the component you want to edit
2. Click the edit (pencil) icon
3. Make your changes in the editor panel
4. Click "Save Changes" when you're done

### Reordering Components

1. In the Content Manager, hover over the component you want to move
2. Use the up and down arrows to change the component's position

### Deleting Components

1. In the Content Manager, hover over the component you want to delete
2. Click the delete (trash) icon
3. Confirm the deletion when prompted

### Saving Your Changes

1. After making all your desired changes, click "Save Page" at the top of the Content Manager
2. Your changes will be saved and applied to your website

## Using the Media Library

### Accessing the Media Library

1. While editing content, click "Media Library" when adding or editing image components

### Uploading Media

1. In the Media Library, click "Upload New"
2. Select the file you want to upload from your computer
3. Wait for the upload to complete
4. Your new media will appear in the library

### Selecting Media

1. In the Media Library, browse through your available media
2. Click "Select" on the media item you want to use
3. The selected media will be added to your component

### Managing Media

1. In the Media Library, you can:
   - Search for specific media using the search bar
   - Delete media by clicking the "Delete" button on a media item

## Publishing Your Website

1. From your dashboard, select the website you want to publish
2. Click "Publish Website"
3. Confirm the publication when prompted
4. Your website will be published and made available to the public

## Managing Your Account

### Updating Your Profile

1. Click on your name in the top-right corner
2. Select "Account Settings"
3. Update your information as needed
4. Click "Save Changes"

### Changing Your Password

1. Click on your name in the top-right corner
2. Select "Account Settings"
3. Click "Change Password"
4. Enter your current password and your new password
5. Click "Update Password"

## Getting Help

### Accessing Support

1. Click "Help & Support" in your dashboard
2. You can:
   - Browse the FAQ section
   - View tutorials
   - Contact support

### Contacting Support

If you need additional help, you can contact support by:
- Email: support@websitegenerator.com
- Support ticket: Click "Submit a Ticket" in the Help & Support section

## Tips for Success

- **Choose the right theme**: Select a theme that matches your brand and the purpose of your website
- **Keep content concise**: Use clear, concise language that engages your visitors
- **Use high-quality images**: Good visuals make your website more appealing
- **Update regularly**: Keep your content fresh and up-to-date
- **Preview before publishing**: Always check how your website looks before making it public

We hope you enjoy using the Website Generator Application! If you have any questions or feedback, please don't hesitate to contact us.
